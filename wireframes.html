Home Page Wireframes

Wireframes for Home Page

Hero Section

The top section immediately grabs the user's attention with a full-width hero image or video, offering a striking visual representation that embodies the brand’s identity. The decision to make this section prominent and immersive reflects an understanding of first impressions and their importance in engaging the user.

Navigation

The navigation bar is designed to be accessible yet unobtrusive, allowing users to explore the website without distractions. By placing navigation links intuitively, the design ensures that users can effortlessly locate different sections, reducing cognitive load. The hover effects on these links provide subtle feedback, enhancing usability and visual interaction, which adds a layer of polish and user engagement.

YouTube Video and Text Introduction

Directly below the hero section, I embedded a YouTube video alongside a short introductory text. This video serves as a visual showcase of our work, giving potential clients an immediate sense of our style and capabilities. The decision to embed a video was deliberate: video content allows for a deeper emotional connection and demonstrates the quality of our work in a way that static images can’t. The accompanying text offers a brief overview, ensuring that the context of the video is clear. This setup invites visitors to engage with our content in a way that feels both natural and informative.

Animated Count-Up Number

One of the standout elements on the homepage is the animated counter, which highlights an impressive metric — “1.5k weddings.” I added this dynamic feature to capture attention immediately, presenting credibility and experience without lengthy explanations. The count-up animation draws the viewer’s eye to this figure and lends an element of excitement, giving a sense of scale and impact that static text alone wouldn’t achieve. It creates an interactive feel on the homepage, inviting users to pause and take note.

Client Review Animation

This choice emphasizes a sense of ongoing engagement and adds social proof in a non-intrusive way. Each time the reviews cycle, it subtly reinforces the reputation and feedback without overwhelming the user with lengthy testimonials. The automated transition serves a dual purpose: showcasing multiple perspectives while keeping the layout clean and uncluttered.

Style Guide for website

Style guide

Color Palette

The primary colors in this design are black, white, grey, and yellow. Each color has been carefully chosen for its specific function and role in supporting the site’s visual hierarchy and branding.

Black: Used as a background color on most sections, black provides a modern, sleek look that complements the brand’s focus on luxury and professionalism. Black grounds the visual experience, giving other elements more contrast and emphasis.

White: Primarily used for text and negative space, white enhances readability and provides a strong contrast against the black background. By using white for the main text, I create a clean, high-contrast interface that ensures clarity and elegance.

Grey: Serving as a neutral background on certain text blocks, grey provides a softer, less intense contrast than black. This subtle tone helps to organize content without overwhelming the viewer, and it is mainly applied in section dividers

Yellow functions as an accent color, adding vibrancy and guiding the user’s eye. I’ve chosen yellow as it reflects the brand's lively, engaging nature. It appears in specific elements like the "Red Pocket Media" branding, hover effects on icons, and navigation highlights, creating a sense of cohesion and continuity throughout the interface.

Yellow was chosen over RED to avoid any potentially inappropriate connotations. The gold undertone of the yellow subtly conveys a sense of luxury and warmth, aligning with the brand’s premium offerings and making interactive elements feel inviting.

Typography

The typography reflects a blend of style and readability. The main title font, Italiana, is a serif typeface chosen for its sophistication and ability to capture attention in the hero sections. Paired with sans-serif fonts in subheadings and body text, it provides a contrast that enhances readability while keeping the overall design refined. The balance between serif and sans-serif typography supports a professional tone without being overly formal, fitting for a brand that mixes creativity with service excellence.

Iconography

Icons are minimalist and functional, using simple, line-based designs in black or white. To add a touch of interactivity, the icons turn yellow on hover, making them visually engaging while also serving as a subtle navigational cue. This approach to iconography maintains visual consistency with the overall color palette, ensuring that the icons don’t distract from the main content. Social Media Icons: The social media icons are located in the footer, with a yellow hover effect. This adds emphasis and encourages interaction while remaining unobtrusive.

Navigation Setup

The navigation is set up as a hamburger menu icon in the top-right corner, making the layout clean and minimizing visual clutter. This icon is simple, easy to recognize, and reinforces the site’s modern feel. When users hover over the navigation options, the text or icons highlight in yellow, reinforcing the accent color and providing clear feedback. This setup is ideal for a website focused on visuals, as it frees up space to showcase large, immersive images. The design encourages users to explore the site intuitively, with smooth scrolling between sections.
